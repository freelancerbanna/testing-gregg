How do I to choose the right edition?

intelligentRevenue is available in two editions. If you plan to use intelligentRevenue for a single entity company you should chose the single company edition. If you plan to use intelligentRevenue for a multi-entity company such as one with separate operating division or organizations, chose the multi-entity company edition.

What are the Pricing Options and Length of Contract?

For the single entity company edition, you may subscribe for $500/month paid quarterly. For the multi-entity company edition, you may subscribe for $1000/month paid quarterly. intelligentRevenue is available on an annual subscription basis and is invoiced and paid quarterly. Our client subscription agreements automatically renew for another year unless canceled in writing 30 days prior to the renewal or anniversary date.

What is required before I can use intelligentRevenue?

intelligentRevenue today is integrated with the Pardot Marketing Automation (MA) system and Salesforce CRM system. Both applications are owned by Salesforce. Client may use any version of Pardot and must subscribe or upgrade to the enterprise or unlimited version of Salesforce CRM because custom app development and the web services API are required. If you want to use Pardot MA or Salesforce CRM we can help you implement these robust solutions using our best practices methodology. If you already use another Marketing Automation system or CRM system and would like to benefit from intelligentRevenue please let us know your environment because our development roadmap is driven by customer and prospect requirements.
